Teaching The excelence

No matter the level of your child, We will train and teach him the rigor of this musical art that is Piano. Our passion is to see him improve and surpass himself, much further than he could have thought at his beginnings.

Our Studio

Edward Leung Studio is one of best Piano School of Canada. Teaching at our institute is intended to be both constructive and demanding. We learn much more than just being a musician. We will teach your children rigor, precision, a way of being in the world with elegance, perseverance and creativity. Teaching this art to your child, ensuring his elevation in these subjects is more than our job: It is our passion and our honor.
